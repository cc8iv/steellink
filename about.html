Welcome to STEEL LINK CAT ENTERPRISES

Built on Quality. Driven by Trust.

STEEL LINK CAT ENTERPRISES, established on August 15, 2024 (RC 7834266), is a trusted provider of premium engineering materials and equipment for industries nationwide.

Our services span Building Materials, Earth Moving Equipment Hire, Scrap/Waste Metal Purchase, Waste Management, General Merchandise, and Contract Execution. As dedicated distributors and agents, we deliver only high-quality products that guarantee durability, performance, and value.

Driven by quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction, we ensure every project benefits from our expertise, prompt delivery, and competitive pricing.
